Who We Are

Deegagh Sea Salt is a Granfather and Grandson company that came from our joint love of the sea and fishing.


Founded in 2020, Deegagh Sea Salt is a family business, which was inspired by the long-forgotten tradition of salt production in the Mulroy Bay. In the time-honoured style, we began producing sea salt in our family kitchen. During lockdown we were able to find more companies around Ireland with similar interests that wanted us to stock their products. Helping us garner the sea salt empire we have now.

Through a combination of sustainable traditional and modern techniques we create a great tasting, pure and natural Irish Sea Salt, which is organically hand harvested in small batches using a polytunnel drying system.

From humble beginnings in our family fishing trips, we have seen our sea salt garner worldwide favour by professional chefs, home cooks and food lovers who appreciate the taste and quality of hand harvested sea salt and that extra dimension it can bring to cooking.
